Introduction

Albion Log ("this site") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

This site may collect and store the following information:

  • Local Storage: Search history, favorite players, theme settings, language preferences are stored in your browser. This data is not sent to our servers.
  • Feedback Information: Content submitted through the feedback form (messages, contact information, etc.)
  • Access Logs: General access logs including IP addresses, browser information, and access timestamps

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Albion Online API: Used to retrieve game data such as player information, guild information, and kill logs.
  • Murder Ledger API: Used to retrieve detailed battle data.
  • Google AdSense: Used for advertising. Google AdSense may use cookies to display ads based on user interests.

Cookies

This site and third-party services (such as Google AdSense) may use cookies. Cookies are used to improve website functionality and optimize ad delivery.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

Use of Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving services
  • User support
  • Advertising delivery
  • Analysis of site usage

Disclosure to Third Parties

This site will not disclose personal information to third parties except as required by law.

Disclaimer

This site is not an official Albion Online service and is not affiliated with Sandbox Interactive GmbH. We do not guarantee the accuracy of game data.

Contact
